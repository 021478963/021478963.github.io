Frequently Asked Questions

Where is _____ building?

All campus buildings are located in the heart of downtown Toronto, near Yonge and Dundas.

For a detailed map to all campus buildings, visit this page.

What is a OneCard?

A OneCard is an important tool at Toronto Metropolitan University. It is used to grant:

  • Building access
  • Campus services
  • Campus discounts

To apply for your OneCard, visit the Get Your OneCard page.

Where can I apply for the Winter 2023 term?

Applications for the Winter 2023 term are accepted online. The winter term application process differs from the fall application process.

To learn more, visit the How to Apply for a Winter Start page.

Can I choose a different name?

You can choose your name for TMU services starting from fall 2022. This includes your email address, your zoom name, and your D2L name. However, your legal name will still be shown on external documents.

To update your chosen name, visit the How to Update a Chosen/Preferred Name page.

How can I update my personal information?

You can update the following information yourself:

  • Chosen name
  • Home address
  • Mailing address
  • Email

To change any of the above information, visit the Personal Information page for instructions.

Other personal information changes require a Personal Data Change Form. Visit Student ID and Personal Information for more information.
